Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a global network of disciple-making Christian communities that will impact the nations with the glorious gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

Training the believer to enjoy a personal relationship with God and to serve Him effectively in every sphere of life, transforming communities through the love of Christ.

Get To Know Us

Our Story

Church Gathering Photo
10+ Years of
Ministry

Abba's Heart Ministries was born out of a deep desire to see believers experience the intimacy of God's love and walk in the fullness of their calling. What began as a small gathering of passionate worshippers has grown into a vibrant community of faith that spans across nations.

Founded on the conviction that every believer is called to be a disciple who makes disciples, we are committed to building authentic Christian communities where people can grow in their faith, discover their gifts, and impact the world around them.

Our name reflects our core belief: that the heart of our Heavenly Father (Abba) beats for every person, and through the revelation of His Son Jesus Christ, we can truly know His love and share it with the world.

What Drives Us

Core Values

Discipleship

Grounding believers in God's Word and equipping them to grow in spiritual maturity, becoming effective followers and representatives of Christ in every area of life.

Community

Building authentic relationships where believers can experience fellowship, accountability, and mutual encouragement as one family united in Christ's love.

Worship

Creating an atmosphere of genuine worship where hearts are lifted to God, lives are transformed by His presence, and His glory is made manifest among His people.

Outreach

Extending God's love beyond our walls through missions, community service, and evangelism, reaching the unreached and bringing hope to the nations.

Senior Pastors & Founders

With a burning passion for God's kingdom and a heart for His people, our founders have dedicated their lives to building a ministry that reflects the very heart of the Father. Their journey of faith, marked by obedience and sacrifice, has led to the establishment of Abba's Heart Ministries.

Together, they carry a vision to raise a generation of believers who are deeply rooted in God's Word, passionate about worship, and committed to reaching the nations with the gospel. Their leadership is characterized by humility, love, and an unwavering trust in God's faithfulness.
